Cinnamon Roll Pancakes

Description

These Cinnamon Roll Pancakes combine the soft, fluffy texture of classic pancakes with the sweet spiced goodness of cinnamon rolls. Swirled with a rich cinnamon-sugar mixture, each bite offers the perfect blend of sweetness and warmth.

Ideal for breakfast or brunch, this recipe transforms a traditional favorite into a special treat that's easy to prepare. Whether you're hosting a weekend brunch or just want to make your morning extra indulgent, these cinnamon roll pancakes are sure to impress!

Ingredients

  • Whole Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Brown Sugar
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Let's start by combining all of our wet ingredients in a bowl. 180ml of milk, 1 egg, and 30g of melted butter. Whisk everything together and set aside as we prepare our dry ingredients.
  2. In a seperate bowl combine 120g of flour, 30g of granulated sugar, 10g baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Little by little combine the wet and dry ingredients mixing our batter together. Now we can start working on our filling.
  4. Combine 65g brown sugar, 8g flour, 1 tsp of cinnamon, and 75g of softened butter. You should be left with a paste.
  5. Transfer you filling into a piping bag so we can pipe it on the pancakes later.
  6. Heat a griddle or skillet over medium heat and spray with cooking spray. Pour about 2/3 cup batter into the center of the griddle. Cook pancake untill bubbles start to form. Pipe filling over pancake.
  7. Flip the pancake and cook untill lightly browned and repeat untill the batter is finished.
